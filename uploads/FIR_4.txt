Complainant: Mrs. Sunita Verma
Date of Incident: 03-07-2024
FIR Number: 183/2024
Police Station: Preet Vihar PS, Delhi
Crime Type: Cyber Fraud (IPC 420, IT Act 66D)

Details: On 3rd July 2024, Mrs. Sunita Verma filed a complaint stating that she was defrauded online while attempting to buy home appliances from a Facebook Marketplace seller. She transferred Rs. 15,000 via UPI, after which the seller became unreachable. The profile was later found to be fake. Cyber cell is investigating.
